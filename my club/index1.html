Real Madrid

I support this club since the day I was born.




Real Madrid Club de Fútbol, commonly referred to as Real Madrid, is a Spanish professional football club based in Madrid. The club competes in La Liga, the top tier of Spanish football. Founded in 1902 as Madrid Football Club, the club has traditionally worn a white home kit since its inception.
The club has won La Liga 35 times, the Copa del Rey 20 times, the Supercopa de España 12 times, the Copa de la Liga once, the Copa Eva Duarte once, the European Cup/Champions League 14 times, the UEFA Cup twice, the European/UEFA Super Cup five times, the Intercontinental Cup three times and the FIFA Club World Cup five times.
I like this club because of a player name Ricardo Kaka. I watched his highlights when he used to play for AC Milan on the sport news and since that moment I became a fan. He moved to Real Madrid on 2009 and then I watch a lot of Real Madrid's games just so I can watch him. He then left the club because unfortunately he didn't perform like how hw used to at AC Milan,but somehow my love for the club hasn't changed.
